Letter of Introduction

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my interest in the opportunities within your organization. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in History with a Minor in Information Technology from the University of Arkansas at Little Rock, supported by a strong academic record. I bring a combination of skills in research, information management, and communication.

My experience spans healthcare administration, archival work, and public service, providing me with a well-rounded background suited for detail-oriented and adaptable roles. I currently serve as a Generalist Clerk with the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Arkansas, where I assist with document processing, case management, and public interaction while supporting daily court operations.

Previously, I worked as an Extra Help Assistant at UA Little Rock Health Services, where I communicated with patients, scheduled appointments, and maintained HIPAA compliance. This role strengthened my ability to work in high-volume environments while managing sensitive information with accuracy and professionalism.

In addition, I served as an Archival Fellow at the Clinton Presidential Library, where I contributed to digitization, preservation, and Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) processing. This experience refined my attention to detail, critical thinking, and understanding of records management and accessibility.

Beyond my professional experience, I have contributed to community and educational initiatives, including serving as a judge for the National History Day Regional Competition. These experiences have further strengthened my communication skills and commitment to public engagement and education.

I would welcome the opportunity to contribute my background in history, information technology, and administrative support to your organization. I am confident that my skills, adaptability, and commitment to excellence would make me a valuable addition to your team.

Thank you for your time and consideration. I look forward to the opportunity to discuss how my qualifications align with your needs. I can be reached at (501) 291-8650 or by email at aurieldc@outlook.com.

Sincerely,

Auriel Del Carmen
