Auriel Del Carmen

I am a graduate of the University of Arkansas at Little Rock with a Bachelor of Arts in History and a minor in Information Technology. Connecting people and knowledge through research and technology is what I do. My experience includes archival work at the Clinton Presidential Library, where I contributed to digitization, preservation, and records processing, as well as administrative and public-facing roles in healthcare and the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Arkansas. I have developed skills in historical research, data management, and web development, and I am interested in opportunities that combine humanities with technology.
